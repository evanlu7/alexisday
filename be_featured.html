Reasons to be featured

The Contour of Luxury publication is structured to provide a center stage platform for select brands to share the key points of their products and services that may not otherwise be emphasized or featured by other outside media sources, which typically write what they themselves want to publicize, not what the brand wants to specifically accentuate. This is our differentiator as a blogAzine™, and the benefit to brands that are featured. In essence, as a blogAzine™ we don’t write in the traditional article format, but rather Alexis writes her commencing blogger expressions about a featured person or brand and provides a Q&A opportunity to the individual or brand. Her Q&A is where the individual or brand is given latitude to state what they would like to give prominence to. Alexis provides a certain flexibility to her interviewees to work with her regarding any edits needed in her Q&A to suit their brand’s agenda and that fit within the blogAzine’s™ framework objectives and standards.
